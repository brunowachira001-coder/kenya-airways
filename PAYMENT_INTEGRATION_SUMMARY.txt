💰 HOW CUSTOMERS PAY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Customer Journey:
1. Customer books flight
2. Selects "Mobile Payment"
3. Enters phone number (e.g., 0712345678)
4. Clicks "Pay with M-Pesa"
5. Receives M-Pesa prompt on phone
6. Enters M-Pesa PIN
7. Payment confirmed instantly
8. Booking confirmed
9. Money deposited to YOUR BANK ACCOUNT


🏦 WHERE THE MONEY GOES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Payment Flow:
Customer → M-Pesa → Your Paybill → Your Bank Account

Settlement Time:
- Daily sweep: Money in bank next business day
- Weekly sweep: Money in bank weekly
- Your choice during setup


🚀 NEXT STEPS (Choose Your Path):

PATH A: M-PESA DIRECT (Recommended for High Volume)
⏱️ Time: 2-3 weeks
💵 Cost: KES 5,000 setup + KES 2,000/month
📊 Fees: 0.5% - 1.5% per transaction

Steps:
1. Apply for Paybill at Safaricom
2. Link your bank account
3. Get production API credentials
4. Deploy to production
→ Best for high volume, lowest fees

PATH B: PAYMENT GATEWAY (Fastest Setup)
⏱️ Time: 2-3 days
💵 Cost: Free setup
📊 Fees: ~3.5% per transaction

Steps:
1. Register with Tingg/Pesapal
2. Get API credentials
3. Deploy to production
→ Best for quick launch
